# Cold Email Templates for AI Startup Outreach

## Template 1 — General intro

**Subject:** Quick hello

Hi [Name],

I’ve been following [Company] and like what you’re building around [product/infrastructure area]. I’m reaching out because I’d love to learn more about the problems your team is solving and whether there may be a fit for someone with experience in [relevant background].

I’m especially interested in [specific area: inference, evals, agents, data infra, etc.]. If useful, I’d be happy to share a few thoughts on [relevant challenge] or send a short background note.

Would you be open to a brief chat?

Best,
[Your Name]

## Template 2 — Role-focused

**Subject:** Interested in [Company]’s work

Hi [Name],

I came across [Company] while looking at teams doing strong work in AI/LLM infrastructure. Your approach to [specific product or technical angle] stood out.

My background includes [brief experience], and I’d be excited to contribute to work like [relevant area]. I’m reaching out to see if your team is hiring, or if there’s someone I should speak with about current needs.

Happy to send over a resume, portfolio, or a short note on relevant projects.

Best,
[Your Name]

## Template 3 — Value-first

**Subject:** Idea for [Company]

Hi [Name],

I’ve been looking closely at companies building in AI infrastructure, and [Company] caught my attention because of your work on [specific product].

I had a few ideas around [optimization, evals, developer UX, GTM angle, etc.] that might be relevant as you scale. I’m also exploring opportunities to work with teams in this space and thought it made sense to reach out directly.

If helpful, I can send a concise note with my background and a few concrete thoughts.

Would that be useful?

Best,
[Your Name]
